Operation Tribute
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Tribute' plaque at the Upper School.

Distinguished Alumni Award
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

This year's Reunion Luncheon at the Upper School celebrated the classes ending in
'3 and '8 and included the Distinguished Alumni Award presentation to Paige Cottingham-Streater
'79.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Celebrating 50 Years!
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Special recognition went to our 50th Reunion celebrants from Montclair Academy and
The Kimberley School classes of 1963.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Reunion Cocktail Parties
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

For the first time ever we held reunion cocktail parties around Montclair for those
classes celebrating their 5th, 10th, 15th 30th and 40th reunions. Egans, Halycon
 and 12 West were each packed with alums who were thrilled to reconnect with one
 another. Due to their profound success we think a new precedent has been set for
years to come!
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Hall of Fame Inductions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The All-Class Dinner Dance at Mayfair Farms included the Hall of Fame induction 
of Dr. Wiemi Douoguih '88 and Caitlin Lehmann '01 (posthumously). Alumni and friends
enjoyed catching up with each other and celebrating the HOF recipients.
Click here [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001cJ2WDbQKHZZfswx9ihpTQGNkuYg2S9v7lpqCItgqyUEqDoxMX-EhRs85NkZ_WwJcMpEk9b5jgYTkG8O5f_Bm8-SUtCRJVl4GFeRoVF75WAJboVSxccTumcXiKkleU_PbIpUJVjG4M3W7bVSsfwEVAg==]

Hall of Fame Induction

Homecoming Weekend 2013!
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

MKA's Homecoming Weekend was incredibly successful and, as our photos show, a great
time was had by all!! We hope you'll enjoy the highlights of Homecoming events in
our featured photos and visit the links for additional photos.

The full weekend of alumni events began on Friday night, and we are thrilled to 
report that a total of 525 alumni participated in the various celebrations on campus
and around town, more than ever before.

Capping off the weekend, 50 members of the MKA community including alumni, faculty,
parents and past parents attended a performance of Pippin on Broadway thanks to 
producer and MKA alumnus Peter Stern '65 who provided choice seating at a deeply
 discounted price.

years ending in '9 and '4.
