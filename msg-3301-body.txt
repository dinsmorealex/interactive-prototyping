SPRINGTIME GREETINGS!

Dear Fellow Alums,
Spring has arrived and with it, a bevy of MKA alumni events.  It was good to see
 a number of you at the New York Reception held in the rooftop atrium at the New
 York Athletic Club (thanks to Ray Knox '76 for sponsoring!).  Last week, a reception
was held at the Middle School to launch the public phase of Future Forward [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001TYWhiF41MEd521tFv245SI852oeleizc1_dhgWzu-VmbnCJB7UXBkPDWMvtBrX7kT0bzOdPSnxl7K9FpR3BKSz3OGMa1javt4l50rXStMlxFRNh3hQdHKDGggIIzNVaB],
an unprecedented $30 million endowment and capital campaign with plans to improve
Middle and Upper School Academic Centers, Muenster Field and the Primary School.
  The Leadership phase of the campaign has already raised an impressive $15.4 million.
Some of the best alumni events of the year are coming up fast so take note and make
plans to attend at least one. The Maypole celebration at Brookside with Kimberly
 School Alumnae on May 1 keeps this great tradition alive; festivities begin at 
8:30 a.m. with a continental breakfast immediately following (please RSVP here [mailto:gberra@mka.org]
if you plan on attending).  At this year's Senior Breakfast on May 2 we will bestow
upon Elvoid Christmas the Honorary Alumni of Distinction award in recognition of
 his many years of dedicated service to the MKA community.  For those outside the
area, there will be a Philadelphia reception on May 9.
The Spring Social at Van Vleck House and Gardens on May 15 promises to be a lovely
evening. The Events Committee has been working very hard and no detail has been 
overlooked. New this year is an extended invitation to parents of alumni and their
presence should enhance the evening.  This catered event will include wine tasting,
compliments of alumni-owned Warwick Winery, and tricky-tray baskets offering golf
lessons, Broadway tickets and many other fabulous prizes for you to win.  Don't 
miss this one!  RSVP [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001TYWhiF41MEfEjxPpY6U5acYIFiBC6iEyC1zPdHNGMVMRsFinIhaTxRuHt9oT6Wl3kSO2vUCeKuhBfGGLxIe6reTOmcaO0_JvmNUWeLgQNWaj085mKVkYHEb2StL8_cqGlpEKsboo5xtkw_KrWsPojE4fXhOf3rWQ]
today if you have not already done so.
I know that's a lot to think about, but also look out for Klank Klassic and Alumni
Sports Day on June 22, Greenwich Alumni Regional Reception on June 13, and the 1st
Annual Headmaster's Golf Outing on June 18.
We are looking for one new Alumni Council member for the 2013-2014 school year, 
so if you or someone you know would like to reconnect with MKA, please reach out
 to us immediately.
As always, please support MKA and your fellow alumni and don't be shy about letting
MKA support you.
Respectfully,
Rich Stanton

MKA ATHLETICS

Congratulations to MKA senior Matt Lane who became MKA's all-time leading scorer
 in Boys' Lacrosse history on April 16th in a game vs. Morristown High School.  
Lane currently has 130 goals and 157 assists (287 career points) and was named The
Star Ledger's Player of the Week last week.  Lane broke Craig Fleishman's (MKA Class
of 2000) previous career scoring mark of 152 goals and 132 assists (284 career points)
which at the time made him NJ's all time leading scorer in Boys' Lacrosse.
Congratulations to MKA's Athletic Director Todd Smith who has been selected as the
Essex County Athletic Director of the Year by the Essex County Athletic Directors'
Association!
Congratulations to Ralph Pacifico (MKA Head Varsity Baseball Coach) who captured
 his 400th career victory on April 9 with his team's 14-2 win over Glen Ridge High
School.  This is a remarkable coaching milestone for "Coach P" who is in his 22nd
year at the helm for the Cougars.
