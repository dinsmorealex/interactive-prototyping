REGIONAL EVENT
 Skidmore - New York City [ https://www.facebook.com/SkidmoreNYC ]  
 Alumni Sunset Happy Hour at Pier 66/Frying Pan 
 When :
 Tuesday, June 17, 2014        
 6:00-9:00 pm          
 Where: 
 Frying Pan [ http://www.pier66maritime.com ] 
 Docked at Pier 66 Maritime, which is located on Pier 66a at West 26th Street in the Hudson River Park, three blocks north of Chelsea Piers.
 Cost : 
 Admission is FREE
 Be one of the first 100 people to check in at the Skidmore College table and receive one free drink ticket!  Cash bar & food to purchase.
 RSVP :
Please register online [ http://www.skidmoreconnect.org/FryingPan2014 ]  by Friday, June 13, 2014
Questions?  Please call The Office of Alumni Affairs & College Events at 518-580-5610
 
The Event of the season is back!  Mingle with the Skidmore alumni community and friends for a sunset on the Hudson at the Frying Pan on Pier 66. Enjoy the sights and sounds of New York harbor while sipping cocktails at the Lightship Frying Pan [ http://www.fryingpan.com ] , a historic ship that sank and remained underwater for three years before being raised and restored to her former glory. Pier 66 Maritime is a former railroad barge complete with a caboose, bar and grill where you can enjoy a sunset view rarely seen from Manhattan that you will not want to miss.
 
  ***Free drink tickets for the first 100 Skiddies who check in.  You will also get a green Skidmore sticker so you can easily find other Skiddies.*** 
 Skidmore will have a small reserved section with some seating available starting at 6:00 pm and lasting through the evening. 
 
 DIRECTIONS 
 Frying Pan is docked at Pier 66 Maritime, which is located on Pier 66a at West 26th Street in the Hudson River Park, three blocks north of Chelsea Piers. You can walk west on 26th Street and cross the West Side Highway for access to the Pier.
 Public Transportation 
 Take M23 crosstown bus west on 23rd Street to Chelsea Piers which is the last stop and walk 3 blocks or 3 piers north. You can transfer to the M23 from the C/E subway lines on 8th Avenue and 23rd Street, the 1/9 lines on 7th Avenue and 23rd Street, or the F and Path Trains on 6th, the N/R on Broadway.
 Car or Taxi 
 By taxi, you can only be dropped off on the NORTHBOUND SIDE of the Westside Highway at West 26th Street.  Cross the highway for the entrance to Pier 66 Maritime and look for the large red lightship.
For cars, the closest Parking Garage is in The Chelsea Piers Sporting Complex. Please note that drop off of passengers in the Hudson River Park is prohibited.
