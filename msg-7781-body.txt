SOMETHING BIG IS COMING!

Dear Fellow Alumni,

We've been very busy kicking off the 2014 - 2015 school year! Autumn is my favorite
time of year, and as the beautiful fall weather is rolling in, be sure to look for
some new and exciting alumni events that will be rolling in with it.

Friday, October 17 is MKA DAY - a brand new way to engage alumni using various avenues
of communication including texting, videos, social media, emails and phone calls.
 We've made it easy and convenient to give to MKA, down to a one-touch screen on
 your smart phone! Currently, we fall below the average percentage of alumni participation
in peer schools, and I know we can do better!  Please participate by pledging or
 donating on MKA DAY... any amount is greatly appreciated.  With at least 300 participants
donating any monetary amount, a $20,000 challenge match will be released from an
 anonymous donor - so we're counting on you!

Next on the calendar is Homecoming Weekend,  October 25th - 26th. We have a full
 weekend of events planned including our traditional Reunion Luncheon for the '4's
and '9's where we will acknowledge our Distinguished Alumni Award recipient Susan
Schadler '69.  Cocktail Parties will be held at local venues for reunion classes,
their friends and families, and the weekend will end with a special Legacy Brunch
for  legacy families.  All alumni are invited to come out and support the Cougars
in a full day of sporting events.

Look out for our Networking event on Tuesday, November 11th.  We've structured the
evening to be like a trade show - attendees will have the opportunity to speak with
different alumni representatives in all fields of interest.  We're hoping this will
benefit those of you seeking a new job, a change, or purely a chance to network 
and see old friends.

And we'll be holding the traditional Alumni Hockey and Basketball Games on November
29th.  We look forward to seeing alumni athletes and spectators relive the good 
old days and celebrate at Tierney's after the games.

I am really excited about our fall calendar and look forward to seeing you at one
of the events.  I hope you'll be involved in MKA DAY, even if only through a click
on your cell phone!  I know you'll find a renewed sense of community and spirit 
of nostalgia when you get back involved with MKA... It's a good feeling!

All the Best,

Jaclyn (Latzoni) Spedaliere '00
