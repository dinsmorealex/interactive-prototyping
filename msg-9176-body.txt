Skidmore - NYC
Ice Skating and Wine Tasting  
  Join Skidmore for two fabulous events... Ice Skating @ Bryant Park and Wine Tasting at Otto. Please see the details below. 
 Ice Skating @ Bryant Park 
  
 Date :        
Saturday, February 21, 2015
 
 When :      
Noon - 2 p.m.
 Please arrive promptly between 11:30 - noon to pick up your passes from a Skidmore   volunteer at the entrance. (Look for a Skidmore sign!)  
 Where :    
Bank of America Winter Village at Bryant Park [ http://www.bryantpark.org ] 
41 West 40th Street, New York, NY 10018
 Cost :        
$9 per adult or child (admission AND skate rental)
$3 per adult or child (admission and NO skate rental)
 Plus VIP access - skip   the line!     
 
Join Skidmore of NYC for an afternoon of fun with friends and family at the Bank of America Winter Village at Bryant Park. This all-ages ice skating event is the perfect opportunity to meet members of the greater Skidmore community while enjoying the outdoors at one of New York City's most popular winter attractions. Take advantage of this discounted event and VIP access- skip the line!
Members of Skidmore's NYC Planning Committee will be present at the check-in booth to distribute wristbands for the rink and skate rentals from 11:30 a.m. to noon. Lockers may be used for free if you bring a lock; otherwise locks are available for purchase for $9.
 
To RSVP or for questions, please contact the Office of Alumni Affairs and College Events via phone at 518-580-5610. 
 
  Please respond on or before February 13, 2015. Advanced registration is required as walk-ins won't be able to be accommodated.   
 
 SkidmoreConnect users may register online [ http://www.skidmoreconnect.org/NYCSkating2015 ] . 
For  directions , click here [ http://www.bryantpark.org/plan-your-visit/hours.html ] .

  Skidmore - New York City  
  Wine Tasting @ OTTO    
 
 Date:  
Wednesday, March 11, 2015
6:30 - 8:30 p.m.
 Where:  
OTTO Enoteca Pizzeria [ http://ny.ottopizzeria.com ]      
One Fifth Avenue
New York, NY 10003
 Cost:  
$50 per person for Young Alumni (2004-2014)
$55 per person for all other class years
(Price includes tasting of two whites, three reds and an assortment of meats and cheese)
 Registration:  
To RSVP or more details, please contact the Office of Alumni Affairs and College Events at 518-580-5610. Skidmore Connect users may register online [ http://www.skidmoreconnect.org/otto2015 ] .
Please register by March 4, 2015.
___________________________________________________________________
Join fellow members of the greater Skidmore community for a relaxing and enjoyable evening at OTTO Enoteca Pizzeria. A creation of chef and proprietor Mario Batali and co-proprietor Jeff Bastianich, OTTO was designed to look and feel like an Italian train station. The name means “eight” in Italian and is located on the corner of 8th Street and Fifth Avenue just steps from Washington Square Park. One of New York’s most celebrated pizzerias, OTTO combines the elegance of a Bolognese enoteca with the fun bustle of an Italian train station.
At this private wine tasting you'll enjoy two whites and three reds along with an assortment of OTTO's amazing meats and cheeses. So, treat yourself and get together with old friends while meeting some new faces too.
