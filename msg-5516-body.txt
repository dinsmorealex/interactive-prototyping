Membership Happenings -- January 2014

-------------------------------------------------

Enjoy Winter in the Best Way Possible: As a MoMA Member!

Happy New Year from MoMA Membership! With extraordinary exhibitions and
members-only events scheduled throughout the months to come, winter is a
fantastic time to be a MoMA member. Take a look at our upcoming member
programs at the Museum and log in to the Digital Member Lounge to
explore brand new original content!

Now through January 12
"Magritte: The Mystery of the Ordinary, 1926-1938"

"Ileana Sonnabend: Ambassador for the New"

Held on the first and third Wednesday of every month at 12:30 p.m.

January 15:
Order and Disorder in Art after WWI

February 5:
"Designing Modern Women 1890-1990"

February 19:
Introducing the Everyday into Art: Neo Dada, Pop, and Experimental Music

in the Museum lobby, starting one hour prior to each talk.

Can't make it to the Museum? Video versions of each talk will be made
available for all members in the Digital Member Lounge.

Exhibition Highlight:
"Isa Genzken: Retrospective"

On view through March 10

Don't miss the first comprehensive retrospective of Isa Genzken's
important, influential, and diverse body of work in an American museum.

Spanning almost 40 years of Genzken's inventive, audacious, and deeply
influential artwork, the exhibition brings together more than 150
objects in an astonishing variety of techniques. The exhibition
encompasses Genzken's more recent assemblage sculptures in addition to
paintings, photographs, collages, drawings, artist's books, films, and
public sculptures--many of which are on view in the United States for the
first time.

View details:
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSxYX8B73jgoB83Mm3Nsl0jb6E/mh185
