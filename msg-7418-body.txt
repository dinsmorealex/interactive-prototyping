REGIONAL EVENT 
 Skidmore - Long Island
 
   A Summer Luncheon
   Sponsored by Mary Hoff Fallon ‘60      
 
  When:         
 Wednesday, August 13, 2014; 12:30 p.m.
 Where:       
  The Devon Yacht Club
300 Abrahams Landing Road
Amagansett, NY 11930
 Cost: 
$30.00 per person; cash bar
$20.00 special pricing for young alumni (2004-2014) 
  
RSVP:          
 To RSVP or for questions, please contact the Office of Alumni Affairs and College Events at 518-580-5610. Please RSVP by August 6, 2014. Skidmore Connect users may register online by clicking here [ http://www.skidmoreconnect.org/longisland2014 ] . 
 
Join Skidmore alumni, parents and friends for an afternoon to enjoy each others’ company and the beautiful beach front setting. A representative from the College will be on hand to give you the latest news from campus. Don’t miss out on this summer gathering! 
 Directions:
 From New York City, take the Long Island Expressway (495) eastbound to exit 70 (Manorville).  Exit 495 heading south on 111.  The road will end at Sunrise Highway, which will have flashing lights and signs automatically directing you onto Sunrise Highway eastbound.  Continue east.  The road changes to County Road 39 and then becomes Route 27.  Follow signs for Route 27 east towards Montauk. 
The town after East Hampton is Amagansett.  When you arrive in Amagansett, there will be a Mobil gas station on your right.  On your left you will see the Amagansett Fire Department and a large white flag pole.  Approximately 300 feet after the flagpole, turn left onto Abrahams Landing Road.  Go over the railroad track and continue straight ahead (you will pass the Lumber Company on your right).  Continue straight and follow the road as it banks to the right.  Stay on this road for two miles.  The Devon Yacht Club will be at the end, on the right on Gardiner’s Bay.
* Please allow extra time for summer traffic.
