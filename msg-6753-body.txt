An Email from Riders Alliance

Relief for G train riders during post-Sandy construction!

Hi Alex, 

More good news for G train riders today. 

When we started our "Improve the G Train" campaign, we asked for more service, better communication with riders, and a free transfer to the J and M trains. 

Last summer, we won progress on service and communication, including those new signs that say where the train will stop. 

Today we're happy to report that we've made progress on the free transfers as well!  After State Senator Daniel Squadron and other elected officials reached out, the MTA has agreed to offer free transfers from the G train to the J and M during G train construction this summer.  See details at http://nydn.us/1jfiDvE

Now we're pushing to make this free transfer permanent, but we wanted to pass along this news in the meantime.

A big congratulations to our G train grassroots leadership team that has worked for almost two years to win all these improvements! 

Best, 

Rebecca Bailin
Senior Organizer
