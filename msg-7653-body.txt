#Skid4Life Weekend starts TOMORROW! 
From August 29 though September 2, Skidmore College is celebrating you and every other Skiddie as part of our first ever #Skid4Life weekend! Whether you’re on or off campus, there are plenty of ways to take part in the festivities, especially on social media. Here’s how we’re making that easy and fun for you:
 
 #Skid4Life Social Media Challenge
 Five days. Five themes. Five chances to win!   
Show your Skidmore pride by participating in a five–day social media challenge. Look at each day’s theme and post a relevant photo on Instagram or Twitter (or through the website [ http://www.skidmore.edu/getinvolved/#form ] ) and  tag it with #Skid4Life . Enter as many times as you want!  The most creative entry from one alum each day will win some great Skidmore swag!
  *Sorry, we can only see, share and consider posts from public accounts.    
 Friday, August 29: College Colors Day
 Challenge: You’re a Skiddie 365 days a year. Wear your yellow, white, and green to show your Skidmore pride today! 
 Saturday, August 30: Mentors Matter
 Challenge: Name or tag your Skidmore mentor on social media (or use our web form [ http://www.skidmore.edu/getinvolved/#form ] ) and tell us how they made a difference in your life.
 Sunday, August 31: Introduce Your-selfie
 Challenge: On convocation day, help us welcome the Class of 2018 by posting your selfie and telling us what you love about being a part of the Skidmore community.
 Monday, September 1: Labor of Love
 Challenge: We know you’re passionate about your career -- tell us why! How did your Skidmore College experience contribute to where you are today?
 Tuesday, September 2: Founder’s Day
 Challenge: We’re bringing back an old tradition. Keep an eye on the Twitter feed for trivia questions about our founder, Lucy Skidmore Scribner. And feel free to hold an off-campus Founder’s Day BBQ with your own Skidmore to-go box! [ http://www.skidmore.edu/alumni/togo.php ]  Put your order in by August 27! 
 Introducing our all new social media page! 
www.skidmore.edu/social [ http://www.skidmore.edu/social/index.php ] 
For #Skid4Life weekend, we launched this all-new custom page [ http://www.skidmore.edu/social/index.php ]  to help keep up with everything Skiddies are sharing across social media! Your posts and photos could show up, too— just make sure you use the hashtag #Skid4Life.  We hope you’ll check back throughout the week to see what’s new here! 
 “I’m not on social media. Can I still share?”  Of course! We’ve built a form on our website [ http://www.skidmore.edu/getinvolved/#form ]  just for you to share your stories, experiences, and photos. 
 What accounts should you follow during #Skid4Life weekend? 
Skidmore College on Facebook [ https://www.facebook.com/SkidmoreCollege ] , Twitter [ https://twitter.com/skidmorecollege ] , Instagram [ http://instagram.com/skidmorecollege ]  and LinkedIn [ https://www.linkedin.com/edu/school?id=19023&trk=edu-cp-title ] .
Skidmore College Alumni on Facebook [ https://www.facebook.com/SkidmoreCollegeAlumni ] . 
For a full schedule of #Skid4Life events, visit the Get Involved  website [ http://www.skidmore.edu/getinvolved ] .
