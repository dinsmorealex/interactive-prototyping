Muenster Field
Building the Future One Brick at a Time
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Dear MKA Family,

I am pleased to write to you as we step into yet another new school year. The fall
is always a time of excitement, anticipation and transformation in schools, and 
that is certainly the case right now on all three of our campuses. In particular,
we are excited to welcome students back to an entirely new playing surface and facility
at Muenster Field. Summer 2013 saw the expansion and resurfacing of the field, as
well as the updating of the parking lot and spectator viewing areas. Our student-athletes
are returning to a first class athletic facility - but we are not finished yet. 
Plans also call for the construction of a field house and pavilion, additions that
will provide changing rooms, restrooms, much needed storage and a fitting entrance
to the field.

We now have the ability to become permanent members of MKA's history in a tangible
way.  As a part of this project, we are providing the opportunity for members of
 the MKA community to purchase engraved paving stones that will be set in the walkways
and pavilion around the field house. With the purchase of a stone, or multiple stones,
you can provide a message cheering on the Cougars, a memorial to a player/players,
relative, coach, or faculty member. You can even immortalize a great moment or team
from MKA's storied past.

But, with the purchase of a paver, you will actually be accomplishing much more.
  You will be funding a project that will impact our entire student body and school.
You will be an integral component of building a facility of which the MKA community
can be proud, as we move into the future.  Please support us in realizing our goals.
Help us recognize our past glory and provide for our future success - you can help
make it happen!
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Go Cougars!

Ken Barrett P '09, '10, '14
