REVENGE OF THE MEKONS!

=C2=A0
Legendary post-punk band The Mekons (
http://www.roir-usa.com/store/tagged/mekons/ )are the subject of Jon
Angio's feature documentary 'Revenge of the Mekons'.=C2=A0 A film that
charts the trajectory of one of the longest-running and most prolific
bands of the first wave of British punk rock bands.
Screenings of the documentary are happening at the Film Forum (
http://filmforum.org/film/revenge-of-the-mekons-film-page ) in
NYC from Oct. 29-Nov. 4!

SONIC HIGHWAYS FEAT. BAD BRAINS!

Fighters 'Sonic Highways' series which premiered their collaboration
track 'The Feast and The Famine'.

SONGS FOR UNSEEN WARHOL FILMS!

=C2=A0
Coming up this week (Nov 6-9) at the Brooklyn Academy of Music (
http://www.bam.org/ )is Songs for Unseen Warhol Films, curated by The
Andy Warhol Museum, featuring 15 never-before-seen, digitally restored
selections from the 1960s are unveiled. Five artists representing a
musical trajectory from the post-Velvet Underground 70s to
today=E2=80=94Dean Wareham (Galaxie 500, Luna), Tom Verlaine (Television) =
(
http://www.roir-usa.com/store/tagged/television/ ),Martin Rev
(Suicide) ( http://www.roir-usa.com/store/tagged/suicide/ ), Eleanor
Friedberger (The Fiery Furnaces), and Bradford Cox (Deerhunter, Atlas
Sound)=E2=80=94perform live alongside Warhol=E2=80=99s celluloid oeuvre, =
featuring
Marcel Duchamp, Edie Sedgwick, Donovan, Warhol himself, and others!
