-- Campus Highlights: D-hall knocks it out of the park! 
 
The tradition continues:  Napkin Notes on the bulletin board in D-hall offer a creative approach to communicating with staff and students. 
[ http://www.skidmore.edu/news/2014/1219-napkin-notes-provide-feedback.php ]
