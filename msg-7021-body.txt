THANK YOU!

Fellow Alums,
It's hard to believe this is my last Cougar Chronicles update. Join me in welcoming
Jaclyn "Jackie" Latzoni Spedaliere '00 as the incoming Alumni Council President.
  I'd also like to thank departing Council Members Cheryl McCants '82 and Mitch 
Decter '94 for their dedicated service to the Alumni Association, and welcome new
Council Members: Brittany Berckes '06, Jonathan Bruno '02, Rich Cosgrove '84, Janine
Garland '82 and Andrew Murphy '01.  I look forward to working with you in the years
ahead!
It felt a bit like winter butted up against summer as Upper School graduation is
 behind us as is Alumni Games Day , the traditional end-of-year alumni event.  For
me, this was my last year of playing; however, I do plan to continue attending the
post-game reception at Tierneys and invite you to join me.  We also had a number
 of great spring events including the New York City Reception hosted at the Friar's
Club by Melissa Cohn '78 and the Van Vleck Spring Social in Montclair.
I had the pleasure of awarding the 2014 Founder's Cup Award to Dr. John Jacobs, 
Latin teacher and 11th grade dean. I don't know if there is a more quintessential
prep school discipline than Latin, and Dr. Jacobs has a knack for instilling a love
of learning among his students.
It has been my distinct pleasure to serve as Alumni Council President largely because
it has helped me reconnect to MKA, the place and the people, in a way I wouldn't
 have otherwise.  Do yourself a favor and put MKA on your list of priorities in 
the coming year. You'll be glad you did!
Have a great summer.
Rich Stanton

DISTINGUISHED ALUMNI AWARD 2014

The Alumni Association's Distinguished Alumni Award for 2014 will be presented to
Susan Schadler '69 at the Reunion Luncheon on Saturday, October 25th.
The award recognizes an alumna or alumnus who has achieved distinction in some field
of endeavor, or through outstanding character or dedication, been of special service
to humanity. The purpose of the award is to bestow recognition and appreciation 
for outstanding achievement, and be a meritorious reflection on the school that 
will provide inspiration and encouragement to its young people.

Ms. Schadler is a well-respected expert in the field of international economic governance.
  Her research in international economic governance builds on her more than three
decades of experience at the International Monetary Fund (IMF). She is now a senior
fellow at a research institute in Canada, the Centre for International Governance
Innovation, where she has written several papers and organized conferences  on sovereign
debt restructuring in financial crises.

HONORARY ALUMNI OF DISTINCTION AWARD

 with you one of the precious gifts of humanity: memories."

FOUNDERS' CUP

Congratulations to Dr. John Jacobs, the 2014 recipient of the Founders' Cup Award,
presented by the Alumni Association to the outstanding faculty member who is most
representative of the best qualities of MKA teaching.  Dr. Jacobs was overwhelmingly
selected by nominations from his peers, students, parents and alumni.  Dr. Jacobs
teaches Latin at the Upper School, is the junior class dean and also a Middle School
soccer coach.
Among the many nomination comments received, the following sentiment was echoed 
by many:  "You cannot find a more caring, devoted individual, both to the subject
and to his students. He is committed to making sure all of his students not only
 know the material thoroughly, but also enjoy learning it. He knows just about everything
about his subject, and is always eager to answer any question you may have. Dr. 
Jacobs' teaching is only rivaled by his kindness, as he is always there to talk 
and offer advice when needed. Overall Dr. Jacobs is an excellent teacher, but he
 has also been a great friend and mentor throughout my time at the upper school."
