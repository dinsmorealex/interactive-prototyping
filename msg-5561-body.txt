MoMA COURSES
-------------------------------------------------

Meet Art and Design Visionaries and More with MoMA Courses

From "psychological cocktails" to modern masterpieces, discover the 
Museum's world of learning through on-site and online courses. 

Sign up before January 22 and get 20% off!
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSyveyB73jgrB83UP8Nsl0jbIl/crss65


ON-SITE COURSES:

Discover art-making courses, artist-led immersions, explorations both
inside and outside the Museum, and more with celebrated artists,
designers, writers, and other expert instructors at MoMA.

Psychological Cocktail Services: Theory and Practice
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSyveyB73jgrB83UP8Nsl0jbIl/crss62#course1

Learn to create cocktails based on works in MoMA's collection with
artist and expert cocktail maker Paco Cao, who will give a guided tour
of the Museum's avant-garde masterpieces and lead a workshop on how to
make psychological cocktails.


Silent Sin
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSyveyB73jgrB83UP8Nsl0jbIl/crss63#course203

This compelling course examines the offenses, failings, indulgences,
crimes, and transgressions depicted in silent movies. Films will be
shown with live musical accompaniment, with discussions prior to and
following the screenings.


Paul Gauguin: In Search of Modernism's Origins
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSyveyB73jgrB83UP8Nsl0jbIl/crss64#course204

Considering Paul Gauguin's productive and highly experimental engagement
with printmaking, this class will examine the life and work of this
oft-mythologized (and self-mythologizing), unconventional, and
peripatetic Frenchman.

...AND MORE


ONLINE COURSES:

At home, in the park, or wherever life takes you, MoMA Courses Online
offer inspiring art and adventures led by today's experts. Discover
modern and contemporary art masterpieces--and make some yourself--and
connect with art lovers from around the world. 

Catalysts: Artists Creating with Sound, Video, and Time
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSyveyB73jgrB83UP8Nsl0jbIl/crss66#course1

Experience video, sound, and performance works by well-known artists 
along with commentary from key figures from the art world about landmark 
media art exhibitions at MoMA. Weekly projects with fellow students 
provide hands-on media-making experiences.


From Pigment to Pixel: Color in Modern and Contemporary Art
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSyveyB73jgrB83UP8Nsl0jbIl/crss67#course1.5

The use of color profoundly changed modern art. Find out how plastics, 
optics, and other modern innovations affected the way artists made 
paintings, sculptures, photographs, and design artworks, and create 
your own color experiments.


Modern Art, 1880-1945
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSyveyB73jgrB83UP8Nsl0jbIl/crss60#course5

Get to know MoMA's iconic collection and the famous artists who 
created it. Exclusive videos illustrate modern art, from its 19th-
century origins in Post-Impressionism to the beginnings of the New York 
School, circa 1940.

...AND MORE


Find out more at MoMA.org/MoMAcourses: 
http://enews-moma.org/a/tBSyveyB73jgrB83UP8Nsl0jbIl/crss59


Online Courses are made possible by a partnership with Volkswagen 
of America.
