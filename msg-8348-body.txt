25 November 2014

Dear MKA Families,

My tenth year at MKA has snuck up on me and prompts a bit of reflection on the state
of the school and our aspirations for the near and far term.  Nine years ago MKA
 adopted six strategic goals to "guide the growth of our school and the continuing
and judicious transformation of our human, physical, programmatic and financial 
resources."  As examples, the introduction of Chinese language study and the 1:1
 technology initiative are action steps that were taken to realize 21st Century 
Teaching and Learning (Goal IV).

Our strategic goals include significant aspirations for MKA's facilities and financial
resources, and these ambitions form the basis for Future Forward, the Campaign for
MKA, which was begun six years ago. Future Forward provides a sustained focus and
an accelerated timetable for the transformation of certain facilities at MKA and
 the strengthening of the school's financial resources.  MKA's overall goal for 
the financial components of Future Forward, which includes endowment, facilities
 improvements and annual giving, is 30 million dollars of which 23 million dollars
has been raised to date.

Having completed major facilities renovations and additions to the Middle School
 and at Muenster Field, and with the renovation and expansion of the Upper School
Academic Center and Green Roof underway, our planning for major changes and additions
to the Primary School Campus is entering the phases of concept refinement, fundraising
and final architectural and site design.

If you know Brookside, MKA's Primary School, then you have an appreciation for the
exceptional learning community that has been fostered by outstanding faculty and
 the leadership of Ginger Kriegel.  If you are a family whose association with MKA
began in Middle or Upper School, I want you to know how proud you should be of Brookside
as a standard of educational excellence for the entire school.

Future Forward is about the promotion of educational innovation and excellence Pre-K-12
through the thoughtful use of precious resources generously provided by hundreds
 of donors.  Our students and faculty benefit now, and so will those who come after
them, from the commitment of all of those who have chosen to invest in MKA's present
and future.

Attached is a link to a brief video that captures a slice of what Future Forward
 has accomplished and will achieve.  Our trustees, alumni, parents, students and
 employees support MKA in many essential and indelible ways and it is a joy to thank
you all for your commitment to the school.

Best wishes for your Thanksgiving holiday,

Thomas W. Nammack

Headmaster
