  The Beatles Arrive in America, 1964  
The fourteenth annual concert of music by the Beatles performed by Skidmore students and faculty recognizes the fiftieth anniversary of the arrival of the Beatles in America.
Music that the band released in 1964 included both new material (e.g., “A Hard Day’s Night”) and material they had already released in the UK the previous year (e.g., “I Want to Hold Your Hand”). This year's program can be found HERE [ http://www.skidmore.edu/academics/music/courses/Skidmania/2014-Beatlemore/index.html ] . [ http://www.skidmore.edu/academics/music/courses/Skidmania/2014-Beatlemore/index.html ] 
Join alumni, parents and friends at Midtown's best sports bar to experience this year's Beatlmore Skidmania concert broadcast live from Skidmore's campus! With the second floor reserved for us, there are plenty of televisions ensuring you'll feel just like you're part of the crowd back on campus. But, better yet, you'll be enjoying appetizers and your first drink on us! So, be sure to celebrate the Beatles invasion of the U.S. in 1964 while enjoying this unique opportunity to connect with fellow Skiddies both locally and from campus. Don't miss out on this lively atmosphere! 
There will be a limited amount of t-shirts available to purchase at the event (first come, first serve). The net proceeds from concerts and t-shirt sales benefit both Skidmore Cares [ http://www.skidmore.edu/skidmorecares ]  (serving the greater Saratoga Community) and student financial aid. Last year, Skidmore Cares was able to donate to a hospice, a rape crisis center, food pantries, and other organizations serving the less fortunate among us.
Beatlemore Skidmania on FACEBOOK [ http://www.facebook.com/BeatlemoreSkidmania ]
