 Tony Giacin

 Steve Schwartz 
 
 
 
 Steve Schwartz '80  delivered the keynote address at Skidmore's 26th annual celebration of scholarships last month. A WSPN-radio alum, Schwartz was a morning radio host in Albany, N.Y., and then Raleigh, N.C. He is president of the Reynolds Group [ http://reynoldsgroupradio.com ] , which coaches TV and radio personalities throughout the U.S. His campus stay included a visit with WSPN's executive board and a studio tour with general manager  Gretchen Schwab '14 .
 
 
 
 
 
   
    
 
 
 Elaine Allen

Sloan Foundation Director's Award for her research report [ http://sloanconsortium.org/news_press/january2013_new-study-over-67-million-students-learning-online ]  on the growth of online education.
