SATURDAY, August 25, 10am-2pm // Urban Food Waste + Rescue Workshop //
FREE ( http://www.3rdward.com/urban-food-waste-workshop/ )

Work on an urban food rescue project, with our 3rd Ward fellows,
Brooke Singer and Ricardo Miranda Zuniga, will allow you to connect
into the ethos of fair redistribution. Build a composting bike for a
community garden in Brooklyn, and contribute to a movement of
sustainable urban living. This one day workshop will help you meet
other like-minded eco-friendly folks dedicated to making Brooklyn more
green.

RSVP HERE >>> ( http://www.3rdward.com/urban-food-waste-workshop/ )

TUESDAY, August 28, 7:30 pm // Building Websites for Dummies Book
Launch ( http://www.3rdward.com/david-karlins-dummies-book )

Our very own instructor David Karlins, launches his latest book,
Building Websites All-in-One For Dummies. David's commitment to all
things interweb makes for a lot of interesting conversation, as you'll
see from our interview. (
http://www.3rdward.com/blog/2012/8/6/book-launch-building-websites-for-dum=
mies-w-david-karlins.html
) Learn the latest trends in web design, including HTML5, multimedia
and mobile functionality, and more!

RSVP HERE >>> ( http://www.3rdward.com/david-karlins-dummies-book )

WEDNESDAY, August 29, 7:30 pm // Intro to the NY Art World w/ Krista
Saunders + Curators ( http://www.3rdward.com/intro-to-ny-art-world/ )

Independent curator Krista Saunders (aka The Bespoke Curator)
concludes her Intro to the NY Art World class with a public,
eye-opening panel discussion with a diverse crew of art world
professionals. They'll share their insights and best practices on how
to begin and sustain a career in the New York City art world.

RSVP HERE>>> ( http://www.3rdward.com/intro-to-ny-art-world/ )

THURSDAY, August 30, WIND UP // Small Business Edition (
http://www.3rdward.com/wind-up-upcoming/2012/8/20/august-30-wind-up.html
)

Join us for an illuminating conversation about crowd-funding and the
world of small business. Our special guest Smallknot talks about their
crowdfunding platform that lets us invest in local small businesses in
exchange for goods, services, special perks and benefits. Featuring
3rd Ward Members from two small businesses, A Different Feather and
Susty Party. Get your networking on!

RSVP HERE >>> (
http://www.3rdward.com/wind-up-upcoming/2012/8/20/august-30-wind-up.html
)

MEMBER OF THE WEEK // VICTORIA VALENCIA (
http://www.valencia-designs.com/ )

3rd Ward Member Victoria Valencia, is an artist and builder, who makes
one-of-a-kind pieces of furniture for her line, Valencia Designs. (
http://www.valencia-designs.com/#! ) Her work resurrects fallen tree
slabs and local-milled lumber into butterfly jointed tables and
stools. After working on house remodeling and carpentry, she's devoted
herself to innovations in fabrication, fusing "function through the
lens of her aesthetic inspirations, resulting in a tangible, livable
urban elegance." We're proud to have Victoria here at 3rd Ward.

CHECK OUT VALENCIA DESIGNS HERE >>> ( http://www.valencia-designs.com/
