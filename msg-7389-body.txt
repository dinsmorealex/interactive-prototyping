Hi, Volunteer,

It’s summertime and the living is easy in Saratoga Springs. We know you can’t always come back to visit Skidmore, so we’re bringing campus to you in this newsletter. As a graduate of the last decade, you’ll receive this quarterly e-news in place of  Scope Monthly  (or if you’re sad about that, click here [ http://www.skidmore.edu/communications/monthly ]  to read the past issues). Enjoy!  

 

 What’s Happening on Campus?  

The student garden has a new home in front of Wiecking Hall and a new name—the Skidmore Community Garden. Can ya dig it? [ http://www.skidmore.edu/news/2014/0612-new-name-location-for-skidmore-garden.php ]  Actually, Sustainable Skidmore [ http://www.skidmore.edu/sustainability ]  has been killing it lately, with a new bike share program [ http://www.skidmore.edu/news/2014/0709-improved-skidmore-bikemore-introduced.php ] , geothermal heating and cooling in 40% of campus buildings, and a car-battery recharging station in Palamountain parking lot.

   

 What’s Happening in ’Toga? 

Storefronts on Broadway change as frequently as the decorations at Riggi mansion [ http://www.timesunion.com/local/article/Drama-dogs-and-diamonds-3380035.php ]  and this year was no exception. The parking lot next to Lillian’s? It’s a bookstore now [ http://www.northshire.com/northshire-bookstore-saratoga ] . The “GhettoChopper” is ghetto no more: It’s a movie theater. [ http://www.bowtiecinemas.com/locations/criterion-cinemas-11-btx ]  And right next to it? A store that serves emonly/em cookies and milk [ http://plumdandycookies.com ] —no lie. And what would summer in Saratoga be without the track [ http://www.saratogaracetrack.com ] ? 

 

 Sports Center 

Baseball fans, grab your peanuts and crackerjacks and catch Nick Petrella ’14 [ http://skidmoreathletics.com/news/2014/6/19/BB_0619140330.aspx ]  as he pitches for the Mohawk Valley Diamond Dogs this summer. Petrella was one of 58 student-athletes [ http://skidmoreathletics.com/news/2014/5/8/Gen_0508143454.aspx ]  named to the Liberty League conference’s All-Academic teams this year.

 

 #Skid4Life  

From August 29 through September 2, Skidmore celebrates you and all #SkidKids as part of our  #Skid4Life weekend . On and off campus, there are plenty of ways to take part in the festivities, especially on social media. Here’s how it works. [ http://www.skidmore.edu/getinvolved ]  

   

 On the Road Again 

Did you know that Skidmore hosts, like, 100 regional events per year? We’re coming to Boston and NYC next. Can’t make it? Host your own Skidmore-themed house party (Solo cups optional) with a Skidmore To-Go Box [ http://www.skidmore.edu/alumni/togo.php ] .

 

 We Want You(r Opinion)  

Are we missing something in this newsletter? Let us know! [ http://www.skidmore.edu/getinvolved/newsletter.php ]
