An Email from Riders Alliance

Help us win better options for riders during R and G tunnel construction!

Please join R and G train riders at Borough Hall on Tuesday, July 2nd at 6:15 pm for an emergency strategy meeting when we will plan how we can win better options for riders during tunnel construction. Snacks will be provided.  (www.ridersny.org/calendar)

The MTA will be conducting necessary post-Sandy construction on the Montague and Greenpoint tunnels causing major service disruptions on the R and G train. 

How will this affect you? 

- R train service between Brooklyn and Manhattan will be suspended for 14 months 
- G train service between Brooklyn and Queens will be suspended for 12 weekends this year and for five weeks in 2014. 

There's no time to wait. Changes are coming soon and riders need options! 

RSVP today (www.ridersny.org/calendar) to participate in this important conversation. We'll be meeting in the 2nd floor conference room of Borough Hall, located at 209 Joralemon Street in Brooklyn. 

I'll see you Tuesday, July 2nd!
