 Together, we did it! 
We are so happy to share that  2,005  Skidmore community members made gifts yesterday, exceeding the 2,000 donor goal and making this our most successful 24-Hour Challenge ever!  Alumni parents, friends, faculty, students, and staff came together in an amazing way to support Skidmore, and we are truly grateful.  
The total of these gifts and the Challenge funds combined will provide  $785,000  for students and faculty—what an incredible example of how collective generosity makes a huge difference.
  Thank you to all donors and volunteers who participated such a success.    
Please click here [ http://www.skidmore.edu/news/2014/050214-501challenge-nets-more-than-2000-gifts.php ]  for a detailed story and photo album of the day's events.
