Homecoming FAQs
Why should I come to Homecoming?
There are a lot of activities competing for your time and attention!  Homecoming
 is a wonderful opportunity to reconnect with your alma mater and especially with
those people who helped shape you as an adult.
It's not my reunion year.  Why should I attend the 125th Anniversary Gala?

This year's event will be exciting, elegant and...surprisingly affordable.  Where
else can you get together with your friends and enjoy cocktails, hors d'oeuvres,
 a four-course meal with wine, and Broadway-quality entertainment for such a reasonable
price?

Does my participation really make a difference?
Yes!  We need all of our alumni to stay active in the life of MKA!  We are a community
with a common experience that deserves your continuing participation.  Come out 
on Saturday afternoon and share your enthusiasm with our athletic teams.  Share 
your memories and achievements with your classmates.  Best of all, enjoy a day at
a place you love.
How can I be sure that my classmates are attending?
The quickest and easiest way to see who is coming to Homecoming is to check with
 our Alumni Office.  Lois Montorio or Gretchen Berra are here to help you. Contact
Lois at 973-509-7939 or lmontorio@mka.org and Gretchen at 973-233-3014 or gberra@mka.org.
You can also check online through Facebook using Montclair Kimberley Academy Alumni
as the key words.
I can't join you but I'd like to make a gift in support of my class.  How can I 
do that?
MKA gratefully accepts cash, checks, credit cards, negotiable securities and, in
 some cases, other types of assets.  The simplest and most direct way to support
 MKA is with an online gift via our secure form at www.mka.org/giving. [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001oOo4gcH1Elzj2Hf4Nq-Azr5xdjLXrMsw3fEqqsQXWFMZF-Ne0iwAD5n54Y9N_EvylfYwNp_1rfdAwdxjJYlHeB5s5t1NXaAEktIeQ7xpIFsgfHrtiWJTbA==]
