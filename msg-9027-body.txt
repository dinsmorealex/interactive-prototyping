NEW YORK THRASH!

The NYHC book by Tony Rettman (
http://www.bazillionpoints.com/shop/nyhc-new-york-hardcore-1980-1990-by-to=

scene spanning over a decade from 1980-1990.=C2=A0
It's an oral history featuring 100's of interviews with members of The
Stimulators ( http://www.roir-usa.com/store/tagged/the-stimulators/ ),
Bad Brains ( http://www.roir-usa.com/store/tagged/bad-brains/ ),
Nihilistics, Heart Attack, False Prophets, Kraut, The Mad, Adrenalin
O.D, Even Worse, Undead all of whom appear on the classic and
definitive=C2=A0ROIR ( http://www.roir-usa.com/news/ )compilation 'New
York Thrash'.=C2=A0  ( http://www.roir-usa.com/store/tagged/va/ )
