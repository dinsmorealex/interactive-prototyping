  DISCOUNTED TICKET INFORMATION

  Programming highlights featuring Skidmore Community 
 Saturday, June 14, 2:15 p.m.  at the Tang Teaching Museum & Art Gallery
Artists' Talk and Reception (FREE and open to the public)
Alumni Invitational 4 art exhibition [ http://skidmore.informz.net/z/cjUucD9taT00MDY3MDg3JnA9MSZ1PTEwNDM2NjgxNTEmbGk9MjMxNzYyMTU/index.html ]  at the Tang Museum featuring  Gayle Wells Mandle ’63, Grace DeGennaro ’78, Nicole Parcher ’90 and Courtney Mattison ’08 

 Saturday, June 14, 8-9 p.m.  at the Universal Preservation Hall, 25 Washington St
No. 11 Productions in Coosje (KOH-shuh)
ARTSPASS or single event ticket (tickets can be purchased at the door)
No. 11 Productions [ http://skidmore.informz.net/z/cjUucD9taT00MDY3MDg3JnA9MSZ1PTEwNDM2NjgxNTEmbGk9MjMxNzYyMTY/index.html ] , a New York City-based troupe of Skidmore alums including  Ryan Emmons '08 and Julie Congress '08. 

 Sunday, June 15, 11 a.m.-12 p.m.  at the Zankel Music Center, ELM Room 117
Sport as Art
ARTSPASS or single event ticket (tickets can be purchased at the door)
“Sport as Art,” is a program led by Skidmore professor  Jeffrey Segrave.   Segrave, who will be joined by college and professional athletes for his presentation, is a noted expert on the Olympic Games and on the impact of sports on society.

 Sunday, June 15, 2-4 p.m.  at Saratoga Arts, Dee Sarno Theater, 320 Broadway
Screening of "Fame High"
ARTSPASS or single event ticket (tickets can be purchased at the door)
Directed by Academy Award nominee and Skidmore alumnus  Scott Hamilton Kennedy '87 , the film depicts the trials and tribulations of young art prodigies attending the famous Los Angeles County High School for the Arts.   Join Scott for discussion and Q&A after the film. 
 
Other highlights are the acclaimed Martha Graham Dance Company [ http://skidmore.informz.net/z/cjUucD9taT00MDY3MDg3JnA9MSZ1PTEwNDM2NjgxNTEmbGk9MjMxNzYyMTg/index.html ]  at the Saratoga Performing Arts Center and Grammy on Thursday, June 12.

 Admission to most festival events is free with the purchase of a SaratogaArtsFest ARTSPASS . For some of the major events, the ARTSPASS provides an admission discount. For more information or to purchase an ARTSPASS, go to www.SaratogaArtsFest.org [ http://www.SaratogaArtsFest.org ] .
  We hope you can join us for SaratogaArtsFest 2014!
