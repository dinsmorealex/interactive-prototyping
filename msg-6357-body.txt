An Email from Riders Alliance

Hi Alex,

The state budget is complete, and the news isn't great for transit riders.

Governor Cuomo tried to take $40 million away from dedicated transit funds during budget negotiations, and we fought back hard. In the end, we were only able to save $10 million from this raid on public transportation.

But we made progress.  Thousands of riders signed our petition against the raid. We made a strong case in the press that Governor Cuomo's raid would hurt riders and won the support of newspapers throughout the region (ridersny.org/inthenews). We rallied with our allies in the state legislature, and both the State Senate and Assembly took our side in their budget resolutions. 

And saving $10 million is a start.  It just isn't enough. 

We need to be stronger next time around.  Please join the Riders Alliance as a member at ridersny.org/member.  Join the fight for better transit.  Help us win better service and more public investment in transit.
