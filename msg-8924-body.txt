MKA ALUMNI + MKA SENIORS = MAY TERM

One of MKA's special programs for senior students is called May Term. After completing
their academic work, seniors pursue an area of interest in one of the following 
areas: international travel, a teaching internship, a career-based internship, community
service, a special project, or work in an MKA-based startup. Throughout the month
of May, May Term reflects MKA's highest aspirations for each graduating class: it
offers them some of the freedom they will experience in the future, asks them to
 take responsibility for their own learning, and implores them to engage with the
world.
