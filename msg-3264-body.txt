April 19, 2013

Dear Members of the MKA Community,

I am excited to be writing to you today to announce the public launch of Future 
Forward: The Campaign for MKA. With a goal of raising $30 million (of which $15.4
million has already been secured) Future Forward is a comprehensive and ambitious
philanthropic campaign designed to create the very best learning environment for
 current and future generations of MKA students.  If the phrase "learning environment"
sounds a little too abstract, consider it as the combination of great programs, 
teaching, resources and students. Earlier this week, some of us had the privilege
to see and hear this combination in action at a performance by the Middle and Upper
School Jazz Bands - an exceptional concert made possible by hundreds of hours of
 practice and rehearsal, skilled direction and teaching, a passion for excellence
from students and faculty, and the resources: facilities, instruments, materials
 and time to develop students into musicians and to transform those musicians into
a first-rate ensemble.

Future Forward is a historic undertaking that arose from the minds and expertise
 of our faculty, our administrators, our trustees, architects and advisors.  We 
have developed plans and made many choices during the course of the past six years
to identify the have to haves for MKA's future.  I believe what you will see in 
Future Forward is a powerful combination of strategic and essential goals.  Our 
goals touch every campus and many of the essential resources of the School, including
Academic Centers for the Upper and Middle Schools that combine the latest research
into libraries, technology centers and academic support, a renovated Muenster Field
to meet the needs of our athletic program, an expanded Primary School, an enhanced
endowment and an increased annual fund to support the excellence of teaching and
 learning for which MKA is known.

I invite you to find out more about this remarkable campaign. Click here [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001kYbQmckTgbC_mJ1BYx4DHc1p_yMhU3NpaX6JriobHJH4P0uHJALgyjlNZ90GRJvhFlclBhFEay9neHprZ0_Y3C6TB99kGJrI2xE33LfONpgL5O7Im54Z3YuiqiiD6hn1]
to read more about our goals, our Campaign Committee and our projects, and click
 here [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001kYbQmckTgbCmtqmReomfQmq5dzoSixx6CuZJFAOzu-Uh2wqFlcJRb1RqYH_plqkA2LJ10qbDSOD02DEhWlaptb0MoOBNVuV7_B7MPPIHPGPTIIrfMygtwrv-qoB7J5Ilu__qJ2C-DYb_IKwvJW70QBQ7znIlVjFrLiZv3WzaX0Q=]
to download the full Case Statement.

I am proud to be here at this time and place in MKA's history and excited about 
the work that lies ahead to realize the aspirations of Future Forward.  My hope 
is that all of you will support this Campaign and be advocates for its goals and
 vision.

Warmest regards,

Thomas W. Nammack

Headmaster
