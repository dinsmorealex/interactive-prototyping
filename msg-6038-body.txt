Skidmore of New York City [ http://www.facebook.com/SkidmoreNYC ]   
  invites you to a   
  Tour of the New York Public Library  
 
 When : 
Saturday, April 5, 2014 at 11:30 a.m. 
 Docents will meet the group at the Information Desk in Astor Hall (first floor).  
Please arrive at least 10 minutes prior to the start of the tour.
The tour will last 1 hour.
  
  Where : 
The New York Public Library [ http://www.nypl.org ] 
Stephen A. Schwarzman Building
Fifth Avenue and 42nd Street
New York, NY 10018    
 
  Cost : 
$7 per person 
  Space is limited, register today!     
 
  Registration : 
Online via SkidmoreConnect [ http://www.skidmoreconnect.org/NYCLibrary2014 ]  or call the Office of Alumni Affairs and College Events at 518-580-5610
 
Join Skidmore of New York City for a tour of the New York Public Library. Arguably the city’s greatest cultural building of all and surely its most beloved, it looks almost exactly as it always has. However, with discussion of a $300 million renovation in the works, it's also been the center of much controversy as to what should or should not be done to this more than 100-year-old building. Come and enjoy a guided tour with fellow Skiddies of one of New York's most impressive structures. This is one event you surely won't want to miss! 
 
 Directions: 
For a map and directions, click here [ http://www.nypl.org/locations/tid/36/directions ] .
