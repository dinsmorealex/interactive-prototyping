Dear Alex,

As we end this program year and head out into the summer, we want to take a moment
to thank you for contributing to all of the Printshop's creativity and success this
year.

The Keyholder and Studio Rental residents received more exhibitions, career development
workshops, and networking opportunities than ever. Five Special Editions and Publishing
residents pushed the boundaries of the studio and had our printers head-scratching
for the full year with their unconventional ideas. Their works are on view through
July 14 with a beautiful (and free!) catalogue available to you. The essay, by Deborah
Cullen, Director and Chief Curator of Wallach Art Gallery at Columbia University,
masterfully documents the work of Alison Elizabeth Taylor, Sebastiaan Bremer, Joell
Baxter, Jonggeon Lee, and Steven Millar. Our expanded printing team--Erik Hougen,
Keigo Takahashi, and Laura Charlton--also provided Contract Printing services for
a variety of projects, and led custom workshops in various techniques for visiting
groups of students, collectors, and corporate retreats. Many of the artists we supported
received major recognition this year including Jennie C. Jones and Hank Willis Thomas.

In all, we provided studio and printing services for over 140 artists, many of them
returning throughout the year. And we are grateful to have worked with each and 
every one.

We received tremendous support for our work from our institutional funders, including
prestigious grants from the Bloomberg Philanthropies and Ford Foundation / Good 
Neighbor Committee. We also presented a successful (and new!) Benefit Sale where,
for two weeks, we offered gorgeous prints at accessible prices and in the best of
company.

So, thank you indeed for your generosity, support, and for visiting often! You have
helped us foster the careers of so many artists and enrich the lives of our audiences.
We look forward to sharing even more with you next season, including this summer.
In the meantime we hope you have a wonderful holiday!

Sincerely,


Dusica Kirjakovic
Executive Director
