ALUMNI NETWORKING SERIES

The first two events of our Networking Series have been great successes. Alumni 
Jason Late '91 and Mark McGowan '85 spoke on December 5, 2012, about their personal
journeys in the finance industry, offering our guests tips for success and taking
questions from the audience.
Both speakers credited MKA as being the cornerstone upon which they built their 
success.  Mark McGowan said, "MKA is a really special place.  It taught me so much
and represented so many different things to me.  It was a place where I learned 
a lot,
where I made friendships that will last a lifetime. It prepared me for challenges
I would face in life, taught me to take responsibility for my actions, to be a leader
and to try to be a positive influence on others. I feel extremely fortunate to be
where I am today."
The second Networking event was held on Wednesday, February 6, on the topic of Integrated
Media, Marketing and Technology with alumni guest speakers     Ryan Schinman '89,
President and CEO, Platinum Rye Entertainment, and Benjamin Aronson '99, Digital
 Leader and Integrated      Marketer. The sold-outaudience listened intently as 
both speakers talked of their successes as well as mistakes made along the way. 
 The value of establishing personal relationships was a critical theme for both 
speakers. The audience was encouraged to follow their passion and not be afraid 
of taking a few steps back in order to advance in their careers.
