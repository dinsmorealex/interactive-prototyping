Emilio Perez is known as a painter's painter, combining the inherent aspect=
s of=20
painting with drawing in his unique stylistic process. His work is a combin=
ation
 of the spontaneity and expressiveness of painting (purposefully in its loo=
sest=20

then going back into the painting to "draw" with a blade cutting his marks.=

rs like
the fluidity of ocean currents.
