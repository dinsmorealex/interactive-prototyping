Dear Alex,

 Thank You!  The exciting Million Dollar Match [ http://www.skidmore.edu/fop/million ]  is gaining huge momentum thanks to our generous donors who have already contributed more than $350,000 in Friends of the Presidents support.  Your commitment helps make a meaningful difference for Skidmore students and faculty, and the College is grateful for your leadership.

 15 Days and Counting... 
A generous group of donors have offered $500,000 in matching funds for the Million Dollar Match and there is still approximately $150,000 remaining. With just a couple weeks to go, please join in—make your FOP gift or pledge today.   

 By making an early commitment, you will help Skidmore receive $1 million to benefit students and faculty. FOP gifts, pledges, and monthly installment gifts committed during the Million Dollar Match and fulfilled by May 31, 2014 will be included. 

To make a pledge, you can contact Lindsey Sadonis directly by e-mail [ mailto:lsadonis@skidmore.edu ]  or call (518) 580-5626.

 The Impact of FOP Support 

Every year, FOP donors contribute more than 85 percent of the total dollars raised for Skidmore, and help the College provide meaningful learning experiences for current students:   

  - A Skidmore education is accessible to more than 1,000 talented students who rely on financial assistance.

  - FOP support helps recruit and retain world class faculty−86% hold the highest degree in their field,

  - Experiential hands-on learning opportunities for students, including internships and faculty-student collaborative research.

  - Sixty percent of students have an international experience and can choose from 140 study abroad programs.

  - A top-notch athletics program provides opportunities for 385 students−last year thirteen teams made it to post-season competitions.

  - A vibrant student life and campus experience that features nearly 100 clubs and organizations and an active Student Government Association.
