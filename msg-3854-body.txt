HAPPY SUMMER!

Dear Fellow Alums,
MKA's 125th year drew to a close with the ringing of the Montclair Academy bell 
at graduation on June 9. It was a great year which culminated this spring with a
 whirlwind of sucessful events, including the Philadelphia and Greenwich Regional
Alumni Receptions, the Spring Social at Van Vleck House and Gardens, Alumni Games
Day (my last lacrosse game?), and the first annual Headmaster's Golf Outing.
MKA's most ambitious Capital Campaign to date, Future Forward, was officially kicked
off this spring.  The $30 million dollar effort will ensure that MKA remains a preeminent,
independent school, with all three campuses to benefit from major renovation projects.
Additionally, work is well underway on turf and a field house for Muenster Field.
 Learn more here [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001l1ZuR2VEtI7qihHTn9OcIC2P2P_YMnZ_1YCNTUIlrAAn2m7KpDpkK3dPz1MDHPfOZpQC0YPLPq5pyL2UhuHx4xkhvFI0zYEnRXWw1yRrokXJT1oyQmT-gASdyR1EjoXgMqqeSmNlWDA=].
It was a pleasure to present this year's Founders' Cup Award to David Hessler, Upper
School history teacher, at the closing staff meeting. The Founders' Cup recognizes
teaching excellence, past and present, at MKA. With so many worthy candidates, we
can all rest assured that the school is under excellent stewardship.
Please mark your calendars for this fall's alumni activities: the NYC Young Alumni
Social at a spectacular new venue on September 18, and an event-packed Reunion and
Homecoming Weekend, beginning on Friday, October 25 through Sunday, October 27.
I hope you have a great summer, and see you in the fall!
Sincerely,
Rich Stanton
