 24 Hour Challenge for Skidmore
We're asking 2,000 donors to make a difference! 
 
Want to make a big difference for Skidmore while mixing and mingling with fellow Skiddies?  Make a gift of any amount on Thursday, May 1 . If Skidmore receives  2,000  gifts within  24 hours , the College will receive  $500,000  from several donors!

Plus, this is a great opportunity to network with fellow alumni. And be sure to wear your Skidmore gear to show your pride!
Can't make it to the event?
