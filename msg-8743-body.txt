The 4th Annual Evening of Career Transition & Transformation 
 Sponsored by Skidmore of NYC, Alumni Association Board,  
 Office of Alumni Affairs & College Events, and Career Development Center 
 When : 
Thursday, January 15, 2015 | 6:45 - 9:00 p.m.
Full event schedule can be found ONLINE [ http://alumni.skidmore.edu/NYCTT15 ] .

 Where : 130 East 59th Street (between Park and Lexington Avenues), New York, NY 10022
 Registration : Please register by calling the Office of Alumni Affairs & College Events (518-580-5610) or online via Skidmore Connect [ http://alumni.skidmore.edu/NYCTT15 ] .   Space is limited, please register by Friday, January 9, 2015   
Mentor Directory [ https://www.skidmore.edu/career/tt-registration/directory.php?event=newyork ] : this list continues to grow, check back often
Video from 2013 [ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8JJ0r7joyoE ] 
 
Skidmore-NYC [ https://www.facebook.com/SkidmoreNYC ]  invites alumni to participate in an incredible networking opportunity on Thursday, January 15, 2015 from 6:45 - 9:00 p.m. in Midtown Manhattan.
Whether you are transitioning to a new career or you are just starting out, this is a tremendous opportunity to make contacts with working professionals and fellow alumni. Feel free to bring a resume to have it reviewed by Skidmore staff and learn how Career Development can help alumni at every stage of their career.
Participants in this event will have the opportunity to meet alumni mentors with experience in a wide range of fields to receive guidance with career transitions and the search for work. Alumni, parents, and friends of Skidmore will staff tables where they will be available for individual questions about career paths, graduate school, and tips on how to approach career transitions. A truly unique event where alumni mentor fellow alumni! Alumni of all ages are encouraged to attend.
 
 SPECIAL PRE-WORKSHOP:  5:30 - 6:30 p.m. “ENCORE: Discovering the next steps on your career path and the resources to help you along the way.” Join Lauren Sisson, assistant director for alumni career development, for a 50 minute workshop before An Evening of Career Transition and Transformation.  (Pre-registration is required, space is limited.)  
 
 [ http://alumni.skidmore.edu/NYCTT15 ]
