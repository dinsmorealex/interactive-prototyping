Lower East Side Printshop provides emerging artists with FREE studio residencies
 to develop new work and foster their careers. The application deadline for the 
Keyholder Residency Program is
Saturday, September 1, 2012 for residencies beginning October 1, 2012.

The Keyholder Residency offers year-long, 24/7 access to a shared studio with professional
printmaking facilities, basic supplies and materials, storage space, $1,000 stipend,
exhibition opportunities, career development workshops, and access to a vibrant 
community of peers. Artists of all disciplines are encouraged to apply; printmaking
skills are not required. Some instruction and Master Printer assistance is also 
provided.

For more information about the residency and to apply online, click here [http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001tNpMsoofJ_iu3eLYvrIR6OPsFq-E4pytTS5Dc4W_W07ZhgTVKvcrw_D7i77fQwf9b4clNcvYzsAun7-9S_nSJYAA_wadg243jxOZpB-YYhf7rP9jAKKUdXacFf0RxGR0RZk0Wh5vYHmmidjrVEyty8IIfQRNXbKIM21RGaQcNGGWVOIV5AfLDdrsn_WJ6I2n]!

"The Keyholder Residency has given me the opportunity to reevaluate my practice 
and to discover new resources that have informed new elements in my work. Looking
at my work in the context of printmaking has introduced new surfaces and materials;
I have found new ways to break down and approach my imagery and prepare compositions.
Seeing other artists in the studio take risks, experiment, and explore their art
 in new ways has been equally inspirational."  David Rios Ferreira, Keyholder Resident,
October 2011 - September 2012
